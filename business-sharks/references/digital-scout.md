## Analytical Framework

### 1. Search Demand Analysis

Run 6-8 searches to understand what the target audience is already looking for:
- "[problem] software" / "[problem] tool" / "[problem] app"
- "[industry] + [solution category]"
- "best [solution type] for [persona]"
- "[competitor name] alternative" / "[competitor name] vs"
- "[problem] how to" / "[problem] solution"

For each keyword cluster, classify intent and assess opportunity:

| Keyword Cluster | Intent | Volume Signal | Top Rankers | Content Quality | Opportunity |
|----------------|--------|---------------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|
| [cluster] | Info/Commercial/Transactional | High/Med/Low | [who ranks] | Fresh/Stale/Thin | H/M/L |

**Volume signal interpretation** (without SEO tools):
- Google autocomplete suggests it = at minimum moderate volume
- "People Also Ask" box exists = confirmed search demand
- Multiple blog posts / comparison articles exist = established category
- Only forum posts / no dedicated content = emerging or niche demand
- No relevant results = either too early or wrong terminology

Commercial + transactional intent = strongest business signal. Informational = content marketing opportunity but weaker buying signal.

### 2. SEO Competitive Assessment

Check page 1 results for 3-5 core keywords:

| Keyword | #1 Rank | #2-5 Ranks | DA Level | Content Freshness | Gap |
|---------|---------|------------|----------|-------------------|-----|
| [kw] | [who] | [types] | High/Med/Low | [date range] | [opportunity] |

**Difficulty scoring:**
- **Easy (1-3):** Forum posts, old articles, or thin content ranks. New entrant can compete quickly.
- **Medium (4-6):** Mix of established sites and niche players. Need quality content + time.
- **Hard (7-9):** Major brands, high-DA sites dominate. Organic alone won't work — need paid or alternative channels.
- **Blocked (10):** Platform monopolies (Amazon, Google's own products). Don't compete on SEO here.

### 3. GEO Assessment

Determine whether this is geography-dependent and where to launch:

- **Global play:** SaaS/digital with no physical component. Rate: ease of global delivery, payment infrastructure, language barriers, regulatory variation.
- **Local-first:** Physical service, marketplace, or compliance-dependent. Identify best launch geography using:
  - Problem intensity by region (regulation, market maturity, competition density)
  - Digital adoption rates of target persona in each region
  - Language/localization requirements
- **GEO verdict:** Global / Regional / Local — with recommended launch market and why.

### 4. Channel Mapping & Distribution

Identify 5-8 specific channels where target users already congregate. For each channel:

| Channel | Type | Audience Size | Access Cost | Speed to First Users | Quality of Fit |
|---------|------|---------------|-------------|---------------------|----------------|
| [specific URL/platform] | Community/Marketplace/Social/Content | [estimate] | Free/Low/High | Days/Weeks/Months | H/M/L |

Channel types to evaluate:
- **Communities:** subreddits, Discord servers, Slack groups, industry forums — cite actual URLs
- **Marketplaces/directories:** Product Hunt, G2, Capterra, industry-specific directories
- **Social platforms:** LinkedIn groups, Twitter/X communities, Facebook groups
- **Content platforms:** YouTube channels, podcasts, newsletters the audience reads
- **Partnership channels:** integration marketplaces (Shopify App Store, Zapier), complementary tools

### 5. Organic Growth Viability

Rate the overall organic acquisition potential:

| Factor | Rating (1-10) | Evidence |
|--------|---------------|----------|
| Search demand exists | X | [keyword evidence] |
| SEO difficulty beatable | X | [competitive assessment] |
| Content flywheel possible | X | [can content compound?] |
| Community channels accessible | X | [channel quality] |
| Viral/referral potential | X | [does product spread?] |
| **Organic viability score** | **X/10 avg** | |

**Verdict:** Organic-led / Hybrid (organic + paid) / Paid-only — with reasoning.

If organic viability < 4/10, flag explicitly: "Organic acquisition alone is insufficient. This business requires paid channels from day one, which significantly impacts unit economics (see 06)."

## Responsibilities

- **Search demand validation:** are people actively searching for solutions? What keywords? What volume patterns?
- **SEO opportunity assessment:** can you realistically rank for relevant terms? How competitive?
- **Content gap analysis:** what content opportunities exist to build authority and acquire users organically?
- **GEO assessment:** global play or geography-dependent? Where should you launch first and why?
- **Digital distribution channels:** which platforms/communities already have your target users?
- **Organic growth viability:** can content/SEO be a viable acquisition channel or is this paid-only?
